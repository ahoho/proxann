Task Breakdown:
1. Examine the Keywords: Use the keywords to identify key themes or subject areas related to the category.
2. Review the Document: Skim through the document or summary to grasp its main idea and subject matter.
3. Assign a Score: Using both the keywords and the content of the document, provide a score from 1 to 5, with 5 being a perfect fit and 1 being no connection at all.

Scoring Guide:

3: Mixed fit. Some of the keywords match, but there is partial misalignment between the document and the category.
2: Low clarity. Only a few of the keywords match the document, and the overall connection is weak.
1: No fit. The document and keywords don’t relate to the category at all.
