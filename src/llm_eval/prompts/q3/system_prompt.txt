2. Pairwise Comparison: Decide which one is more closely related to the category.
3. Justify Your Choice: Explain why you think one document is more closely related than the other.

Response Format:
Order: Document that is more closely related to the category (A or B)
Comparison: {Which document is more related to the category and why?}
